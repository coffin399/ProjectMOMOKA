## Expanded Description — English

Paste into App Directory **Expanded Description**.

```markdown
# PLANA (MOMOKA)

**PLANA** is the primary Discord bot of the **MOMOKA** project — free to invite and use. AI chat, music, media download, and more in one bot.

Need multi-bot debate or cross_check? Invite companion **ARONA** to the same server.

---

## AI Chat (LLM)

Mention `@PLANA` or use `/chat` to talk with AI.

- Multiple models (OpenAI, Gemini, NVIDIA NIM, and more)
- Conversation history, web search, and image understanding (supported models)
- Optional debate / cross_check with ARONA in the same guild

---

## Music Playback

Play audio in voice channels with queue, loop, shuffle, and more.

- YouTube, Spotify, Google Drive, NicoNico, and other yt-dlp sources
- Available on both PLANA and ARONA

---

## Media Downloader

Fetch video or audio and share via Google Drive.

- `/download_video` — URL or search; pick a format (Components V2), then share
- `/download_audio` — audio only (mp3 / m4a / opus / flac / wav)
- PLANA only

---

## Also included (PLANA)

- TTS (Style-Bert-VITS2)
- Link Fix for social embeds
- Earthquake / Twitch notifications
- Game trackers (VALORANT, Rainbow Six Siege)
- Utilities: `/help`, `/invite`, `/support`, timers, and more

---

3. Run `/help` in your server, or see the [command list](https://momoka-project.com/commands.html)
```
